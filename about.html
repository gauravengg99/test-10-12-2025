About Gaurav Engineering

Established in 1983, Gaurav Engineering stands as a pioneering force in the plastic recycling industry. We began our journey as a consulting firm in plastic extrusion and have evolved into a specialized, world-class manufacturer exclusively dedicated to plastic recycling machinery.

Our Legacy

Over four decades, we have built a rich history of designing and manufacturing extruders for diverse applications including:

  • Tape lines and Blown film production
  • Monofilament and Pipe extrusion
  • Solid Rod and Artificial hair
  • Ball pens and Box strapping
  • Profile and Compression molding for both plastic and rubber

Our Vision Today

Today, Gaurav Engineering is exclusively focused on delivering comprehensive end-to-end recycling solutions. Leveraging decades of expertise and consulting knowledge, we provide machinery across all four critical recycling processes:

  • CRUSH – Advanced size reduction and shredding
  • WASH – Dry and wet cleaning systems
  • EXTRUDE – Pelletization and compounding
  • PALLETIZE – Strand and die-face cutting

Capacity Range

We deliver solutions for 25 Kg/Hr to 1000 Kg/Hr capacity, catering to startups, mid-sized operations, and industrial-scale recycling plants.

Global Reach

With 40+ years of experience and a team of 32+ skilled technicians, we have established ourselves as trusted partners for plastic recycling solutions worldwide.

Our Commitment

  • Quality assured machinery with 100% testing before delivery
  • Expert technical support from our team of 32+ skilled technicians
  • Innovative solutions tailored to your recycling needs
  • Reliable partnership for long-term business success

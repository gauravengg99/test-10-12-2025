About Us

Established since 1983, Gaurav Engineering is a leading provider of end-to-end plastic recycling solutions for both post-consumer and post-industrial waste.

What started as a consulting firm in plastic extrusion has evolved into a specialized, world-class manufacturer exclusively focused on plastic recycling machinery. We have a rich history of designing and manufacturing extruders for tape lines, blown film, monofilament, pipe, solid rod, artificial hair, ball pens, box strapping, and profiles.

Today, we deliver comprehensive recycling solutions from 25 Kg/Hr to 1000 Kg/Hr across all four formats:

  • CRUSH – Size Reduction & Shredding
  • WASH – Cleaning & Drying Systems
  • EXTRUDE – Pelletization & Compounding
  • PALLETIZE – Strand & Die-Face Cutting

FOUNDER & VISIONARY

Meet Nitin Patel, Founder & Managing Director of Gaurav Engineering. Since establishing Gaurav Engineering in 1983, Nitin Patel has been the driving force behind our evolution from a consulting firm in plastic extrusion to a world-class manufacturer of comprehensive plastic recycling solutions. With over 40 years of industry expertise, he has guided the company to achieve a dominant 70% market share in the Indian FIBC recycling sector. Under Nitin's visionary leadership, Gaurav Engineering has pioneered end-to-end recycling solutions (CRUSH–WASH–EXTRUDE–PALLETIZE) serving startups to industrial-scale operations with capacities from 25 Kg/Hr to 1000 Kg/Hr. His commitment to quality, innovation, and customer-centric service has established the company as the trusted partner for plastic recycling machinery across India and globally, with over 700+ installations. "The future of plastic recycling lies not just in the machinery we build, but in the sustainable impact we create. At Gaurav Engineering, we believe that every machine we deliver should not only process plastic efficiently but also contribute meaningfully to a circular economy. Our commitment to excellence, backed by 40+ years of experience and a team of dedicated professionals, ensures that your investment in our solutions is an investment in sustainable growth and operational excellence."

Nitin Patel Founder & Managing Director, Gaurav Engineering
